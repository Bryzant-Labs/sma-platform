  • CORO1C overexpression rescues SMA in zebrafish (Wirth Lab, PMID 27499521)
  • PLS3-CORO1C interaction: STRING-DB confidence 0.818
  • SMA motor neurons may be attempting to compensate via CORO1C — needs larger dataset confirmation
  • ESM-2 analysis: CORO1C is a structural outlier among SMA targets
The Untested Hypothesis
Nobody has measured CORO1C expression after HDAC inhibitor treatment in SMA cells.
  • LBH589 (panobinostat) produces ~10-fold SMN increase in SMA fibroblasts
  • miR-133a-3p directly represses CORO1C (PMID 25518741)
  • HDAC inhibitors modulate miRNA expression profiles
  • If HDAC inhibitors also upregulate CORO1C: dual therapeutic mechanism (SMN + modifier)
